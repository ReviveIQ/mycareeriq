# Bryan Pipeline - User Guide

Welcome to Bryan Pipeline, your AI-powered job search management system. This guide will walk you through all features and help you maximize your job search efficiency.

## Getting Started

### 1. Dashboard Overview

When you first log in, you'll see the main dashboard with key metrics:

- **Total Prospects**: Number of companies in your pipeline (30 by default)
- **High Priority**: Companies requiring immediate outreach
- **Remote Roles**: Number of remote-friendly positions
- **Key Contacts**: VPs and Directors identified for outreach

### 2. Navigation Tabs

The main navigation includes five tabs:

| Tab | Purpose |
|-----|---------|
| **Pipeline Table** | View and manage your 30-company prospect list |
| **Analytics** | Visual insights into your pipeline composition |
| **Generate** | Create and send AI-powered applications |
| **History** | Track all sent applications and outcomes |
| **Settings** | Customize job research preferences |

---

## Pipeline Table

### Viewing Your Prospects

The Pipeline Table displays all 30 companies with the following information:

- **Company**: Company name and link to job posting
- **Category**: Business category (e.g., "Revenue Intelligence", "Sales Enablement")
- **Role**: Target job title and description
- **Stage**: Current stage (Research, Outreach, etc.)
- **Priority**: High, Medium, or Low priority
- **Key Contact**: Hiring manager name and LinkedIn profile
- **Remote**: Whether the role is remote-friendly
- **Est. Comp.**: Estimated compensation range
- **Links**: Quick links to job posting and LinkedIn profile

### Filtering & Searching

**Search**: Use the search bar to find companies by name, contact, or job title

**Stage Filter**: Filter by pipeline stage (Research, Outreach, Negotiation, etc.)

**Category Filter**: Filter by business category

**Priority Filter**: Filter by priority level (High, Medium, Low)

### Exporting Data

Click the **Export CSV** button to download your entire pipeline as a CSV file for external analysis or sharing.

## Generate & Send Applications

This is the core feature of Bryan Pipeline. Follow these steps to generate and send applications:

### Step 1: Select a Company

1. Click the **Generate** tab
2. Click the dropdown menu labeled "Choose a company from your pipeline..."
3. Select a company from the list

The selected company details will appear:
- Company name
- Target role
- Hiring manager name and LinkedIn profile
- Job description and company profile

### Step 2: Generate Documents

1. Click the **Generate Cover Letter & Resume** button
2. Wait 10-15 seconds for AI to generate personalized documents
3. Review the generated content in the preview dialog

The AI will generate:
- **Cover Letter**: Personalized for the specific role and company
- **Tailored Resume**: Customized to highlight relevant experience

### Step 3: Review Generated Content

The preview dialog shows two tabs:

**Cover Letter Tab**:
- Personalized greeting to the hiring manager
- Compelling narrative about your interest in the role
- Relevant experience and achievements
- Professional closing

**Tailored Resume Tab**:
- Your resume tailored to the job description
- Relevant skills and experience highlighted
- Quantifiable achievements emphasized

### Step 4: Verify LinkedIn Profile

1. Enter the hiring manager's LinkedIn profile URL
2. Click **Verify LinkedIn Profile**
3. The system will extract the contact name for verification

This step helps ensure you're sending to the correct person and enables LinkedIn-based follow-up.

### Step 5: Send Application

Choose your sending preference:

**Send Immediately**:
- Application sent to hiring manager right away
- Email copy sent to your account
- PDF attachments included with both documents

**Schedule for Later**:
- Select a date and time for sending
- Application will be sent automatically at the scheduled time
- Useful for strategic timing

### Step 6: Confirmation

After sending, you'll see a success message:
- "Application Sent!"
- "Your cover letter and resume have been sent to [Hiring Manager]"
- "A copy was also sent to your email"

The application will now appear in your **Application History** with status "Sent".

## Application History

### Viewing Sent Applications

The History tab shows all applications you've sent:

| Column | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| **Company** | Company name and role |
| **Contact** | Hiring manager name and LinkedIn profile |
| **Status** | Draft, Scheduled, Sent, or Failed |
| **Outcome** | Pending, Interviewing, Offer, or Rejected |
| **Sent Date** | When the application was sent |
| **Actions** | View, edit, or delete application |

### Updating Application Outcomes

As you progress through interviews, update the outcome status:

1. Find the application in the History tab
2. Click the **Outcome** dropdown
3. Select the current status:
   - **Pending**: Awaiting response
   - **Interviewing**: In interview process
   - **Offer**: Offer received
   - **Rejected**: Application rejected

This helps you track your progress and identify which companies are most interested.

### Filtering Applications

Use the search and filter options to find specific applications:
- Search by company name, contact, or job title
- Filter by status (Draft, Sent, Scheduled, Failed)
- Filter by outcome (Pending, Interviewing, Offer, Rejected)

### Viewing Application Details

Click on an application row to see:
- Full cover letter text
- Full resume text
- Hiring manager information
- Sent date and time
- LinkedIn profile link
- PDF storage keys (for archival)

## Settings

### Job Research Preferences

Customize how Bryan Pipeline researches new job opportunities:

**Enable/Disable Research**:
- Toggle daily job research on or off
- When enabled, 30 new roles are researched daily at 8 AM EST

**Research Time**:
- Set the time for daily research (default: 8 AM EST)
- Research runs automatically at this time

**Roles Per Day**:
- Number of new roles to research daily (default: 30)
- Higher numbers provide more opportunities but may require more review

**Job Categories**:
- Select which business categories to research
- Focus on categories relevant to your target roles

**Keywords**:
- Enter keywords to include in job research
- Examples: "SaaS", "Enterprise", "Account Management"

**Exclude Keywords**:
- Enter keywords to exclude from job research
- Examples: "Startup", "Freelance", "Contract"

### Email Digest

Configure your email digest preferences:

**Digest Frequency**:
- Receive digest every 24 or 48 hours
- Digest includes newly researched opportunities

**Digest Content**:
- Number of opportunities per digest
- Job categories included
- Formatting preferences

## Email Delivery

### How Applications Are Sent

When you send an application:

1. **Cover Letter PDF**: Generated and uploaded to secure storage
2. **Resume PDF**: Generated and uploaded to secure storage
3. **Email to Hiring Manager**: Sent with both PDFs as attachments
4. **Email Copy**: Automatic copy sent to your email for record-keeping

### Email Content

The email includes:
- Professional greeting to the hiring manager
- Brief introduction and interest statement
- Mention of attached cover letter and resume
- Your contact information
- Professional closing

### Tracking Email Delivery

The application history shows:
- **Sent Date**: When the email was sent
- **Sent to Hiring Manager**: Confirmation that email was delivered
- **Sent to User**: Confirmation that copy was sent to your email

### Troubleshooting Email Issues

**Email Not Received**:
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Verify hiring manager email address is correct
3. Check email delivery logs in Settings

**PDF Attachment Issues**:
1. Verify PDFs were generated successfully
2. Check file size (should be < 5 MB)
3. Try resending the application

## Daily Job Research

### How It Works

Every day at 8 AM EST (or your configured time):

1. **Research**: System researches 30 new job opportunities (or your configured number)
2. **Analysis**: Each opportunity is analyzed for fit and relevance
3. **Pipeline Update**: New opportunities added to your pipeline
4. **Notification**: You receive a notification about new opportunities
5. **Email Digest**: New opportunities included in your next digest email

### Customizing Research

In the Settings tab, customize:
- **Categories**: Which business categories to research
- **Keywords**: What to search for
- **Exclude Keywords**: What to avoid
- **Roles Per Day**: How many opportunities per day

### Reviewing Research Results

New opportunities appear in:
1. **Pipeline Table**: Added to the main prospect list
2. **Analytics**: Updated in pipeline composition charts
3. **Email Digest**: Included in your digest email

## Email Digest

### What's Included

Every 48 hours (or your configured frequency), you receive an email digest containing:

- **New Opportunities**: Recently researched job opportunities
- **Company Summaries**: Brief overview of each company
- **Role Details**: Job title, description, and compensation
- **Contact Information**: Hiring manager details
- **Quick Links**: Direct links to job postings and LinkedIn profiles

### Using the Digest

The digest helps you:
- Stay informed about new opportunities
- Quickly identify high-priority prospects
- Plan your outreach strategy
- Track pipeline additions

### Digest Frequency

Configure how often you receive digests:
- **24 Hours**: Daily digest with new opportunities
- **48 Hours**: Every other day digest (default)
- **Weekly**: Weekly digest with all new opportunities

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Application Generation Fails**
- Ensure company information is complete
- Check that job description and company profile are populated
- Try again in a few moments

**Email Not Received**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify email address is correct
- Check email delivery logs

**PDF Storage Issues**
- Verify PDFs were generated successfully
- Check file sizes are reasonable
- Try resending the application

**Job Research Not Running**
- Verify research is enabled in Settings
- Check that research time is set correctly
- Ensure timezone is set to EST

### Getting Help

If you encounter issues:
1. Check the Settings tab for configuration options
2. Review this User Guide for common solutions
3. Check the browser console for error messages
4. Contact support with details about the issue

## FAQ

**Q: How long does application generation take?**
A: Typically 10-15 seconds for both cover letter and resume generation.

**Q: Can I edit generated cover letters and resumes?**
A: Yes, you can review and edit content in the preview dialog before sending.

**Q: How many applications can I send per day?**
A: There's no limit. Send as many as you'd like, but quality is more important than quantity.

**Q: What happens to my sent applications?**
A: They're stored in the Application History with full details, PDFs, and outcome tracking.

**Q: Can I schedule applications for specific times?**
A: Yes, use the "Schedule for Later" option to send at a specific date and time.

**Q: How often should I update application outcomes?**
A: Update as soon as you hear back from companies to keep your pipeline accurate.

**Q: Can I export my pipeline data?**
A: Yes, use the Export CSV button to download your entire pipeline.

**Q: How does daily job research work?**
A: Every day at 8 AM EST, 30 new job opportunities are researched and added to your pipeline.

**Q: Can I customize job research?**
A: Yes, in the Settings tab you can customize categories, keywords, and number of roles per day.
